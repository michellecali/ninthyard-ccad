What is CleanCoreTM Technology?

CleanCoreTM is an innovative cleaning technology that infuses cold water with ozone gas to create aqueous ozone, a natural and robust cleaning solution. CleanCoreTM eliminates the need to store and use most chemical cleaners. The unique properties of CleanCoreTM help to control and even eliminate odors.

CleanCoreTM is delivered onsite and on demand through the CleanCoreTM Sanitation System (CCT). CCT is a compact system that only requires power and a cold water supply to deliver unlimited quantities of CleanCoreTM when and where you need it most.
